GET TO KNOW ME GAME

This Game is so that you can get to know me a little bit better.

Here are the steps to the game below. I will ask you some yes or no questions about me.

  • First Question: Do you know Trey?
  • Second Question: Would you like to meet him?
  • Third Question: Does Trey like sports?
  • Fourth Question: Would Trey jump out of an airplane?
  • Fifth Question: Was Trey a sponsored athlete?
  • Sixth Question: How many different towns has Trey lived in while in Washington?
  • Seventh Question: Name a state that Trey has lived in other than Washington?
